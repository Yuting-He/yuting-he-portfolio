    State and NUTS-3 district indices are area-weighted from the underlying sub-basin predictions.

    Spatial decision model

    One hydrological model, three decision scales.

    01

    Screen 614 sub-basins

    DWD ICON maximum, minimum and apparent temperature, VPD, precipitation, FAO ET0, and 3-81 cm root-zone soil moisture feed each daily basin index.

    02

    Aggregate to 400 local regions

    Sub-basin predictions are weighted by exact EPSG:3035 intersection area into NUTS-3 districts and urban districts, then rolled up to the 16 states.

    03

    Gate role-specific actions

    Transparent urban/rural sensitivity assumptions shape the impact lens; stale inputs suppress prompts and consequential decisions require human verification.

    Open-data architecture

    A production-shaped separation of geometry, forcing, model, and action.
