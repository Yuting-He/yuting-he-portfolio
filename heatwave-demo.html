    Spatial decision model

    One hydrological model, three decision scales.

    01

    Predict in 614 sub-basins

    Synthetic temperature-, thermal-stress-, soil-water-, SPI-, ET-, vegetation-, and low-flow-like proxies are generated for each scenario day.

    02

    Aggregate to 400 local regions

    Sub-basin scenarios are weighted by exact EPSG:3035 intersection area into NUTS-3 districts and urban districts, then rolled up to the 16 states.

    03

    Gate role-specific actions

    Synthetic exposure assumptions change the impact lens; all role prompts remain low-regret evidence checks with explicit human verification.

    Open-data architecture

    A production-shaped separation of geometry, forcing, model, and action.
